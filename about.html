About the Catalogue

Jazz and its related genres are my favourite types of music. I created this site so I can share my interest in jazz and album recommendations with others. For a full list of albums in plain text, along with any other (non-jazz) albums in my music collection, click here.

I am always looking to broaden my musical horizons, so I would be glad to hear your listening suggestions. Feel free to contact me through my main website.

Notes and Omissions

A fundamental characteristic of jazz is improvisation, so attending live events is highly recommended.

My jazz library has a lack of various periods and sub-genres, including early jazz (eg. pre-30s, ragtime, New Orleans, dixieland), vocal jazz, and post-80s genre jazz (eg. avant-garde, free jazz, fusion). There are also very few modern/active musicians.
