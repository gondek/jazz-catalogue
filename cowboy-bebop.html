Cowboy Bebop

Seatbelts

Cowboy Bebop is a very popular anime which has received critical acclaim for its character design, art, voice acting, style, thematic elements, and of course, music. The (first) soundtrack album is largely jazz-based, but also contains some country and blues songs. Be sure to listen to the additional soundtrack albums if you like the mixture of styles or if you liked the show. Some songs I would strongly recommend are: “Tank!” (the opening theme song), “Bad Dog No Biscuits” (intense ska jazz), “Space Lion” (“space-blues” & tribal fusion), and “Car24” (a light hearted tune).

Also check out this live Seatbelts performance, which is very intense and a lot of fun, but perhaps a little bit overdone? Watch it and decide for yourself!
