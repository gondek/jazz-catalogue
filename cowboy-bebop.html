Cowboy Bebop

Seatbelts

I think it's time to blow this scene. Get everybody and their stuff together. Okay, three, two, one let's jam!

Tim Jensen's spoken intro on "Tank"
  • Wikipedia Article
  • AllMusic Review
  • Listen on YouTube

With no disrespect to the fans of the critically acclaimed anime, I could imagine a situation where the music for Cowboy Bebop was written first, and then the show was put together after the fact. The (first) album is largely jazz-based, but also contains some country and blues songs. Be sure to listen to the additional soundtrack albums if you liked the mixture of styles or if you liked the show.

Also check out this live Seatbelts performance, which is very intense and a lot of fun, but perhaps a little bit overdone? Watch it and decide for yourself!
