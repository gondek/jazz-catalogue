Cookin' with the Miles Davis Quintet

Miles Davis

After all, that's what we did - came in and cooked.

Miles Davis
  • Wikipedia Article
  • AllMusic Review
  • Listen on YouTube

This album is part of a group of 4 (the others being Relaxin’, Steamin’, Workin’) that were recorded in two days (May 11 and October 26, 1956) by Miles’ “First Great Quintet”. It’s amazing how so much great music can be recorded in so little time, a testament to the ability of the group and the tightness between its members.
